WINTER SEASON

Winter is the coldest season of the year, occurring between autumn and spring. It is characterized by shorter days, longer nights, and often brings snow and ice in many regions.
People typically wear warmer clothing such as coats, hats, and gloves to protect themselves from the cold temperatures.
Winter is also a time for various activities such as skiing, snowboarding, and holiday celebrations.

Activities in Winter

  • Skiing and Snowboarding
  • Building Snowmen
  • Ice Skating
  • Holiday Celebrations
  • Drinking Hot Beverages

Conclusion

Overall, winter is a unique and enjoyable season that offers a variety of experiences and activities for people to enjoy despite the cold weather.
